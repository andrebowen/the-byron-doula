Services

Personalised support for pregnancy, birth and early parenthood.

Birth Preparation

Feeling prepared makes all the difference. Whether you're a first-time parent or going into birth again with new questions, my birth preparation support gives you the knowledge, tools and confidence to meet your birth on your own terms.

In-person in Byron Bay and the Northern Rivers, or virtually from anywhere.

  • Birth Planning: Build a clear, realistic birth preferences guide
  • Comfort Techniques: Positions, breathing, movement and coping strategies
  • Partner Preparation: Help your partner understand their role
  • Information & Education: Evidence-based guidance at every stage
  • Emotional Readiness: Process fears and arrive at birth feeling grounded
  • Flexible Format: Single session or a series

$150/hr · Virtual & in-person available

Parent Coaching

Parenthood raises questions that don't always have easy answers. My coaching sessions offer a calm, non-judgmental space to work through whatever you're navigating — pregnant, postpartum, or early years.

All sessions held virtually — connect from the comfort of your home, wherever you are.

  • 1-on-1 Sessions: Dedicated time focused entirely on you
  • Birth Planning Support: Clarify your options and build a plan
  • Postpartum Questions: Feeding, sleep, recovery and emotional adjustment
  • Relationship & Partner Dynamics: Support for couples stepping into parenthood
  • Tailored to You: No generic advice — shaped around what you need
  • Ongoing Support: Single sessions or a coaching package

$150/hr · Virtual only · Single session or package
