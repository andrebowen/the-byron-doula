Birth Preparation

Feel informed, confident, and ready for birth.

A personalised 2-hour session that gives you and your partner the knowledge, tools, and confidence to approach birth feeling calm and prepared.

Whether you're planning an intervention-free water birth, an epidural, or a cesarean, this personalised session will give you and your partner everything you need to approach birth with clarity and confidence. Drawing on 13 years of experience supporting families in the birth room, I'll help you prepare for the best possible birth experience for your unique circumstances.

Sessions are available in-person in Byron Bay, or virtually from anywhere in the world.

What's Included

  • Personalised Birth PlanHelp you communicate your preferences and have productive conversations with your care provider
  • Labour Comfort & Breathing TechniquesHelp you work with your body and navigate labour with greater confidence
  • Partner PreparationYour birth partner knows exactly how to support you when it matters most
  • Personalised Postpartum PlanEnsure you have the practical and emotional support needed for a smoother transition into parenthood
