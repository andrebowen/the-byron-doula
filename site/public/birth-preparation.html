Birth Preparation

Feel informed, confident, and ready.

A personalised 2.5-hour session giving you and your partner the knowledge and tools to approach birth with calm and clarity.

Your birth, your way

Whether you're planning a water birth at home, or a scheduled caesarean, this session is built around your specific circumstances. Drawing on 13 years of experience supporting families in the birth room, I'll help you prepare for the best possible experience, whatever that looks like for you.

What's Included

  • 2.5 hour session
  • Personalised birth plan
  • Labour comfort and breathing techniques
  • Medical & non-medical pain relief options
  • Partner preparation
  • Optimise birth outcomes & minimise interventions
  • Postpartum Plan
