Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This privacy policy describes how The Byron Doula (Nathalie Solis Pérez) collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number and the content of messages sent through this website's contact forms.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To deliver requested resources (such as the free birth guide)
  • To arrange and manage doula services
  • To send occasional relevant updates (you may unsubscribe at any time)

Data Storage

Form submissions are processed by FormSubmit and delivered to our email. We do not store payment information on this website.

Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Law you have the right to access, correct or request deletion of your personal information. Contact us at hello@thebyrondoula.com to exercise these rights.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email hello@thebyrondoula.com.
