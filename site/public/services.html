Birth Preparation

Feeling prepared makes all the difference. Whether you're a first-time parent or going into birth again with new questions, my birth preparation support gives you the knowledge, tools and confidence to meet your birth on your own terms.

Sessions are available in-person in Byron Bay and the Northern Rivers, or virtually from anywhere.

What's Included

  • Birth PlanningBuild a clear, realistic birth preferences guide tailored to your wishes
  • Comfort TechniquesLearn positions, breathing, movement and coping strategies for labour
  • Partner PreparationHelp your partner understand their role and how to support you
  • Information & EducationEvidence-based guidance on your options at every stage
  • Emotional ReadinessProcess fears, set intentions and arrive at birth feeling grounded
  • Flexible FormatAvailable as a single session or a series of sessions
