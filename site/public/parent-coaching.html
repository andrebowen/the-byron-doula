Parent Coaching

Less power struggles.
Better sleep.
More connection.

A personalised coaching session grounded in Aware Parenting, giving you practical tools to respond with confidence and strengthen your connection.

Whether you're navigating tantrums, challenging behaviour, sibling conflict, big feelings, or sleep struggles, this personalised coaching session will help you understand what's driving your child's behaviour and give you practical tools that work.

Grounded in Aware Parenting, you'll learn how to respond with confidence, strengthen your connection, and support your child's emotional wellbeing, without punishments, rewards, or bribes.
