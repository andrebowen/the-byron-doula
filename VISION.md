# VISION — The Byron Doula Website

## What This Site Is

A trust-first conversion website for Nathalie, a certified holistic birth doula and aware parenting coach based in Byron Bay. The site's one job: turn a curious visitor into an enquiry.

The decision to contact a doula is emotional, not rational. Visitors need to feel safe, seen, and confident before they act. Every design, copy, and UX decision serves that emotional arc.

---

## The Client

**Nathalie** — The Byron Doula  
13 years experience. Byron Bay + Berlin. Certified Holistic Birth Doula. Aware Parenting Coach. In-person (Byron Bay / Northern Rivers) and virtual worldwide.

**Positioning:** Premium, experienced, calm, evidence-based. Not clinical. Not generic.  
**Tone:** Warm, personal, non-judgmental. First-person where possible.

---

## The Audience

Expecting parents and new parents who:
- Are making a high-trust, emotionally significant decision
- Need to feel confident in the person before they reach out
- Are often anxious — the site should calm, not overwhelm

---

## Conversion Goal

One primary action: **submit the enquiry form.**

No product purchases. No direct booking. No secondary CTAs competing with the enquiry.

---

## Page Structure (locked)

Homepage follows this exact trust-to-action flow:

```
Header → Hero → Trust Bar → About Preview → Featured In →
Services → Reviews → FAQ → Contact Form → Footer
```

This is not negotiable without Andre's approval. The order is strategic.

---

## Design Direction

- **Design system:** Earth & Grace (warm earth tones, natural textures, premium whitespace)
- **Feel:** Calm, grounded, premium — not medical, not trendy, not cold
- **Typography:** Refined serif for headings, clean sans for body
- **Photography:** Real, warm, natural light — no stock photo aesthetic
- **Mobile-first** — the majority of visitors will be on phone

---

## Fit Criteria for Autonomous Decisions

An agent can proceed without asking if the decision:
- Supports the trust-first emotional arc
- Reduces friction toward the enquiry form
- Stays within the Earth & Grace design system
- Preserves the locked page structure
- Uses copy from `site-text.md` as the source of truth

An agent must ask Andre if the decision:
- Adds a new page or removes an existing one
- Changes the homepage section order
- Introduces a new CTA or conversion path
- Requires new copy not in `site-text.md`
- Changes pricing visibility or service descriptions
- Alters the brand positioning or tone
