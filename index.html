Everything You Need to Know

A doula provides continuous emotional, physical and informational support during pregnancy, birth and the postpartum period. I help you feel prepared and confident, support your partner, and advocate for your birth preferences — so you can focus entirely on your experience.

Absolutely. Midwives and OBs focus on your medical care. A doula's role is entirely different — I'm there for continuous, uninterrupted emotional and practical support, birth preparation, and to help you navigate decisions with clarity. We work alongside your care team, not instead of them.

I'm based in Byron Bay and serve families across the Northern Rivers region of NSW. I also offer virtual support for birth preparation, parent coaching and postpartum check-ins for families further afield.
