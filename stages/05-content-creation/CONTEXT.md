# Stage 05 — Content Creation

## Purpose
Write and prepare all website content.

### Activities
- Copywriting
- Photography
- Video preparation
- SEO optimization
- Testimonials and case studies

### Deliverables
- Final approved content

Many projects actually do this alongside design rather than after it.

## Inputs
- `stages/01-discovery-strategy/output/intake.md` and content outline (from Stage 01)
- Approved designs / prototype (from Stages 03/04)

## Outputs
- Final approved copy for all pages
- Optimized images, videos, media assets
- SEO metadata, alt texts, etc.

## Process
1. Write or refine all page copy based on strategy and designs.
2. Prepare photography and other media.
3. Optimize for SEO.
4. Collect and format testimonials/case studies.
5. Get client approval on all content.
6. Update `../../MEMORY.md` and move to development.

## Notes
Content can (and often should) run in parallel with visual design and prototyping.

## Checkpoint
All content final and approved before or alongside development.
