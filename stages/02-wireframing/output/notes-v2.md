# Notes — Wireframe V2

If Nathalie's positioning is "premium, experienced, calm, evidence-based support," this structure supports that message very well and avoids many of the common issues service-based websites have (too much text too early, weak trust signals, or scattered calls-to-action).

One final refinement I'd consider when you move from wireframe to UI design: make the Trust Bar, Featured In, and Review Summary visually connected. Together they create a powerful credibility stack: experience → recognition → client outcomes. That combination will likely be one of the strongest conversion drivers on the site.

One final UX note: I would consider changing the Reviews section and Featured In section order to:
Reviews
Featured In
FAQ
as shown above. Visitors typically trust other parents' experiences more than media mentions, so leading with testimonials often has a stronger impact than leading with publications. The current structure already reflects that.

↓

↓

↓
Proof
↓

↓

This is generally a stronger structure for a high-trust, relationship-based service such as doula support, birth preparation, and parent coaching.

## Visual Treatment (UI Design)

Trust Bar
Featured In
Review Summary

could be visually connected.
That doesn't necessarily mean they need to be physically adjacent in the page structure.
For example, during UI design you could give them:
The same background colour
The same card style
The same iconography
Similar spacing
Similar typography treatment
So users subconsciously group them as:
Credibility
even though they appear in different locations.

## Final CTA + Contact — Merged

The CTA and contact section were asking the user to do the same thing, one after the other. As a visitor, it felt like:

"Ready to enquire?"
↓
"Ready to enquire?"

**Fix:** Merged into a single section.

**Heading:** Let's Talk About Your Journey

**Subheading:** Ready to feel more informed, supported and prepared?

**Body:** Whether you're expecting your first baby or navigating a new chapter of parenthood, I'd love to hear more about where you are and how I can support you.

**Reassurance:** No obligation. I'll personally respond within 1 business day.
