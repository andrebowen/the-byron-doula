Design Notes

What changed from the current palette

  • Secondary was amber gold (different hue). Now it's muted taupe — same hue as primary, chroma dropped from ~36 to 16. More understated, lets primary lead.
  • Tertiary was near-duplicate of old secondary. Now it's olive/sage (hue 118.8°), generated by +60° rotation from primary. Grounds the warm palette with an earthy, botanical note.
  • Surface family is essentially unchanged — hue-biased warm neutrals driven by the same source hue.
  • The tertiary-container sage (#e1e7b1) appears in testimonial backgrounds and card accents — the green reads as natural, not clinical.
