Scenario H — Horizontal Cards · Sans-Serif Test

Type scale (all Inter): 11px labels · 13px features · 14px body · 20px card title · 28px section title · 40px price. No serif anywhere.

  • Price at 40px/700 still dominates the card footer — the weight does the work the serif size was doing before.
  • 20px/600 card title is cleaner and reads faster than 30px serif at 400 weight.
  • Body copy and features sit at consistent 14px/13px — easy to scan, nothing competing.
  • Next: decide if this or the serif version feels right for the brand, then pair with a booking section.
