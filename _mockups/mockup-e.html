Scenario E — Chip Selector + Form

How it works: Single price ($150/hr) means no pricing decision to make. The service cards explain each offering — "Book Now" scrolls to the form and auto-selects the right chip. The form itself is just 4 questions: which service, name, email, message. One button.

  • Pros: Absolute minimum friction. No dropdowns, no pages, no drawers. The whole booking flow is visible at once.
  • Pros: "Not sure yet" chip removes the anxiety of having to choose correctly before reaching out.
  • Pros: The form doubles as the contact section — nothing to maintain separately.
  • Cons: No inline payment — sends an enquiry rather than a transaction. Payment follows via invoice or Stripe link.
