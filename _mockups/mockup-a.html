Scenario A — Card Expands In-Place

How it works: "Book Now" expands the card to reveal the booking form inside it. Other cards dim. The service and price are locked in at the top of the form — no dropdown needed.

  • Pros: Zero navigation. The user never leaves the context of their chosen service.
  • Cons: Cards become tall when expanded — can feel cramped on smaller screens. Three cards expanding is chaotic without JS collapsing siblings.
  • Best for: Users who are browsing and then decide in one motion.
