Scenario D — Dedicated Booking Page

How it works: Service cards on the homepage link to booking.html?service=birth-prep-virtual. This page reads the URL param, locks in the service, and presents a clean two-column form with no distractions. The switcher bar at the top simulates different URL params for the prototype.

  • Pros: Maximum focus at the moment of conversion. No competing elements. Shareable URL — Nathalie can send people directly to book a specific service. Bookmarkable.
  • Cons: Navigates away from the homepage. Introduces a second page to maintain. Without URL params, "Book a session" from hero needs a service picker step first.
  • Best for: Direct links from Instagram bio, email campaigns, referrals. Strong complement to any of the other scenarios.
