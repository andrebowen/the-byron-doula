    Scenario B — Select Card, Booking Section Reacts

    How it works: Clicking "Book Now" marks the card as selected and scrolls to the booking section. The left panel confirms the service. The form knows what's been chosen — no dropdown needed.

    • Pros: Clear two-step flow. Single-page. The "↑ Change selection" link makes it trivial to switch. Hero CTA can scroll here in a neutral state.
    • Cons: Requires scrolling. On long pages, the gap between cards and booking section can break the mental link between choosing and booking.
    • Best for: Visitors who browse carefully before deciding.
