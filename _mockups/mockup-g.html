Scenario G — Just the Form

The idea: Remove the service choice from the form entirely. The visitor already knows what they want from the service cards above — they don't need to confirm it again. Name, email, message. That's it. Nathalie can clarify the service in her reply.

  • Pros: Absolute minimum fields. No friction at all. Anyone can fill this in. Works identically whether they came from a specific card or the hero CTA.
  • Pros: Treats the form as a conversation starter, not a transaction — which matches how Nathalie actually works.
  • Cons: Nathalie has to ask "which service?" in every reply if the visitor doesn't mention it — one extra back-and-forth.
  • Verdict: Works well if the free-text message field gets used. Works badly if people just write "hi" and nothing else.
