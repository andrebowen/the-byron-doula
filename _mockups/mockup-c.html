Scenario C — Slide-Out Drawer

How it works: "Book Now" opens a panel from the right edge. The page dims behind it. The service is confirmed at the top of the drawer — no dropdown. The CTAs are sticky at the bottom of the panel.

  • Pros: Clean separation of browse vs book. Familiar e-commerce pattern. Page context stays visible behind the overlay.
  • Cons: May feel too transactional / app-like for a warm, personal service brand. Most JS complexity of any option.
  • Best for: Users who want to compare services before committing.
