About
Nathalie

Certified birth doula, cultural anthropologist, and mother of three — holding space for the most profound threshold of your life.

Her Story

From a Calling to a Career

"I've sat with women in their hardest moments. I'd be honoured to sit with you."

Drawing from my own experience as a mother of three, I can say that an empowered birth is not just good luck — it is helped by deep birth preparation and a support team dedicated to your vision. If we truly started honouring birth and parenthood as the transformative processes that they are, our birthing culture would look very different.

Born in Lübeck, raised in Germany, Guatemala and Spain, matured in the U.S. and Australia, residing between Byron Bay and Berlin. I derive inspiration from many different cultures and traditions.

— Nathalie Solis, The Byron Doula

Guiding Principles

My Philosophy

01

Evidence-Based

I stay current with the latest research so I can offer you accurate, balanced information — helping you make the decisions that are right for your family.

02

Non-Judgmental

Your birth, your choices. Whether you want an unmedicated home birth or a planned caesarean, I'm here to support your vision without agenda.

03

Continuously Present

I don't leave when my shift ends. I stay with you through the whole labour and birth — because consistency of support is what makes the difference.
