About Nathalie

I've sat with women in their hardest moments. I'd be honoured to sit with you.

Drawing from my own experience as a mother of three, I can say that an empowered birth is not just good luck — it is helped by deep birth preparation and a support team dedicated to your vision. If we truly started honouring birth and parenthood as the transformative processes that they are, our birthing culture would look very different.

Born in Lübeck, raised in Germany, Guatemala and Spain, matured in the U.S. and Australia, residing between Byron Bay and Berlin. I derive inspiration from many different cultures and traditions.

Training & Credentials

  • Cultural Anthropology, Masters of Arts, Leipzig University
  • Certified Birth Doula CBI, Childbirth International
  • Certified Doula, Dr. Michel Odent
  • Certified End of Life & Death Doula, Thoughtful Transitions
  • Spinning Babies approach to childbirth
  • Pregnancy & Birth Specialist, Parent Coach at Cleo
  • Certified Aware Parenting Coach, Aware Parenting Institute
  • Possums & Co — Sleep, breastfeeding & unsettled babies
  • Certified Yoga Teacher, BYV
