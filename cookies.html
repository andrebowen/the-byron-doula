Cookie Preferences

How this website uses cookies and how to manage them

Last updated: May 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function correctly and, in some cases, allow the site owner to understand how visitors use the site.

2. Cookies we use

This website uses a minimal set of cookies. We do not use advertising or behavioural tracking cookies.

  • Strictly necessary cookies — Required for the site to function (e.g. form submission). These cannot be disabled.
  • Google Fonts — Loading typefaces from Google may set a short-lived session cookie and log your IP address. See Google's privacy policy.

3. What we do not use

  • Analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels

4. How to manage cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Safari
  • Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Your rights under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold. You also have the right to object to or restrict certain types of processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

6. Updates to this policy

We may update this page as our practices change. The date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
