Brand Principles

Voice & Feel

Every visual decision should reinforce the brand's core values: warmth, expertise, and deep care for families during the most profound moments of their lives.

🌿 Color Personality

  • Warm amber → sunlit warmth, harvest, nourishment
  • Warm brown → grounded stability, earthy linen
  • Sage green → calm nature, gentle growth, vitality
  • Warm cream → softness, comfort, intimacy

✍️ Typography Voice

  • Cormorant Garamond → editorial, feminine, timeless (display only)
  • Inter → accessible, clear, professional (headlines + UI)
  • Serif for display text creates warmth and gravitas
  • Never use all-caps for body text

📐 Design Principles

  • Soft shapes (28px cards) over harsh angles
  • Generous whitespace — breathable, not cluttered
  • Earthy palette — avoid cold blues and grays
  • Photography: authentic, warm light, human

♿ Accessibility

  • #211B13 on white → 17.1:1 (AAA)
  • White on #805610 → 6.4:1 (AA)
  • Focus ring: 3px primary, 3px offset
  • All interactive elements keyboard accessible
